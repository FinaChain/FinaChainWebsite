About FinaChain

FinaChain is the enterprise blockchain that enables businesses to run blockchain applications using smart contracts.

FinaChain is an autonomous network secured by a group of validators. This distributed group of validators allows the network to provide fast and inexpensive transactions.

FinaToken (XFNA) holders can use their tokens to become validator candidates. A minimum of 1 million FinaTokens are required as collateral to become a validator candidate. Every seven days, a new round (epoch) starts, and a new set of validators is selected from the list of candidates.

The validators will add a new block to the blockchain every 5 seconds based on the AuRa blockchain consensus algorithm. AuRa is a Proof-of-Authority consensus engine, and the name originally comes from Authority Round.

What can I do with Fina?

Fina is the native cryptocurrency on the FinaChain blockchain and serves as the base token for any process that takes place on the FinaChain blockchain.

Send and receive Fina and use it to transfer value.

Interact with the blockchain, deploy tokens, tokenized assets, and NFTs on the FinaChain.

Participate in projects deployed on the FinaChain blockchain like token farming or decentralized exchanges.

Build and deploy smart contract-based technologies such as Dapps and DeFi protocols.
